Tsukasa: As luck would have it, we all have plenty of experience performing on this stage.
Tsukasa: Plus, we can use the props and costumes we have right here to put on that show.
Emu: That show?
Tsukasa: Yes. If it's a show the four of us have done before, then we won't need much time to practice...
Tsukasa: Honestly, could you ask for any better replacements than us?
Emu: B-But I invited you guys to Phoenix Wonderland because I wanted you to all have fun and enjoy yourselves...
Rui: That won't be a problem.
Nene: I agree.
Nene: I mean, there really is nothing more fun than us doing shows together.
Tsukasa: And you have guests waiting to see a show, no...?
Tsukasa: Which means we need to make putting smiles on their faces our priority. That is why we perform on stage!
Nene: Let us help, Emu...
Nene: I really want us to do a show together, since it's been so long.
Robo-Nene: I will also help.
Emu: You guys...
