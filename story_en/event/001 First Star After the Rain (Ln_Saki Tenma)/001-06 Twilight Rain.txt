1-6 Twilight Rain

(Character: Ichika Hoshino, Saki Tenma, Honami Mochizuki, Shiho Hinomori, Tsukasa Tenma, Megurine Luka)

[Saturday]

[Saki's Room]

Saki: It's raining.
Saki: My fever's pretty much gone...
Saki: But there's no point in going to the observatory now. We wouldn't be able to see anything when the sky's overcast.
Saki: ...
Tsukasa: Saki, breakfast's ready. I even made you the hot chocolate you like. Will you come down?
Saki: Tsukasa...
Saki: (He took a day off from his part-time job to stay home with me.)
Saki: (That's one more person I've inconvenienced.)
Saki: Sorry, I don't feel like eating right now.
Tsukasa: No? Well, if you get hungry, come downstairs and I'll fix you something up anytime.
Saki: Okay.
(White cut)
(Background change)

(Memory cut-in)

Saki: I...miss them so much.
(White cut)
(Background change)

(Memory cut-out)

Saki: I'm so dumb.
Saki: Everyone's here for me, I should be happy...
Saki: But I wanted to see the stars so much.
Saki: Huh? Was that my phone?
Saki: Ah, that's right. If I go over there, I can see the stars.
(Black cut)
(Background change)

[School SEKAI]

Saki: Hello!
Saki: There's nobody here. Maybe they went to the rooftop?
(Black cut)
(Background change)
Saki: Nobody here either.
Saki: Where did everyone go?
Saki: I ended up alone again...
Luka: Saki?
Saki: Oh! L-Luka!
Luka: You're wearing PJs today? That's a surprise.
Saki: Um, there's a reason for this!
Luka: Is it also the reason why you came here on your own?
Saki: Haha, well...
Saki: It seems you can read me like a book.
Luka: You overestimate me. There's much I don't know about you.
Luka: I could see that your usual energy was gone, though.
Saki: ...
Luka: Would you like to tell me what's wrong?
(Black cut)
Saki: So, you see, it's all my fault.
Saki: I insisted on going to the observatory together, but then I was careless and caught a cold as a result, and ended up just being a bother to everyone...
Saki: I have myself to blame for this.
Luka: Why did you overexert yourself like that?
Luka: As Shiho said, you could go to the observatory some other time.
Saki: That's because...
(White cut)
(Background change)

(Memory cut-in)

(White cut)
(Background change)

(Memory cut-out)

Saki: I've wanted to spend a day out with my friends like that for a very long time.
(Black cut)
(Background change)

[Neighborhood]

Ichika: Saki hasn't replied to me.
Honami: What if she got worse and is staying in bed? Maybe we shouldn't visit?
Ichika: We'll just pop in to say hi and leave her to rest.
Ichika: I think she's feeling very lonely today.
Shiho: Why do you say that?
Ichika: Huh?
Shiho: She was so obsessed with going out today she got herself sick. I don't get her. Do you?
Ichika: Maybe.
Ichika: Something she said a while ago has been nagging at me.
Shiho: A while ago?
Ichika: Soon after she was admitted to the hospital. We once visited her together, remember?
Shiho: Um, yeah? It was a really long way to that hospital.
Honami: Her mom said they put her there because of a famous doctor specialising in her condition.
Ichika: I actually went to visit her many more times after that together with her mom.
Ichika: I remember one of our conversations...
(White cut)
(Background change)

(Memory cut-in)

Saki: Whaaat, our teacher has had a baby?! Wow, that's wonderful!
Saki: I have to write her a letter with my congratulations! Do you mind taking it to her?
Ichika: No problem, I can do that.
Saki: Thanks! Now, where did I put my stationery... Here!
(Black cut)
Saki: ...and please take care. There, done!
Saki: You know what, I'll write to Hona and Shiho too! Letters are more personal than texts.
Saki: They seem so busy lately. They don't reply to my messages much.
Ichika: Um, yeah, they've got a lot going on. Maybe we can all visit you together soon.
Ichika: (That's...not very likely, though.)
Saki: Teehee... I'll draw cartoon pictures of them, too!
Saki: ...
Ichika: Saki? Something wrong?
Saki: ... No.
Saki: I was just thinking that...when I give you these letters, you'll go and see everyone.
Saki: I wish I could see them, too.
Ichika: ...
Saki: Haha, sorry, that sounded pitiful. It's the rain making me moody.
Saki: ...don't forget to write back! There, I finished!
Saki: Hona might write back, but I wonder if Shiho will bother. Hehe.
Saki: Okay, here are the letters. Thank you!
Ichika: No problem.
(White cut)
(Background change)

(Memory cut-out)

Shiho: Saki...
Honami: Poor Saki...
Ichika: She must have been much lonelier than we realized at the time.
Ichika: So I think she'd be happy if we visited her together now.
(Black cut)
