116-8 The Way I Played That Day

(Character: Kanade Yoisaki, Mafuyu Asahina, Ena Shinonome, Mizuki Akiyama, Hatsune Miku, Kagamine Rin, KAITO)

[A few days later]

[Empty SEKAI]

Rin (N25): ...
Miku (N25): Is something the matter, Rin?
Rin (N25): I'm wondering if Kanade's okay.
Miku (N25): Oh, that's right. She visited a little while ago...
Rin (N25): Yeah, she said she was feeling better, but I'm still a little worried...
Miku (N25): I see...
KAITO (N25): ...
Rin (N25): Oh...
Miku (N25): Kanade, you're here.
Kanade: Yes, hello. Mafuyu and the others should also be arriving shortly since I'll have them listen to the new demo today...
Kanade: But before they come, there's a song I'd like for you all to hear...
Rin (N25): Huh? Us?
Kanade: Yes, I'll play it now...
KAITO (N25): ……!
Rin (N25): It feels a little different from your usual songs, Kanade.
Miku (N25): I agree... Normally, I'd feel warm and light after my chest tightened up a little.
Miku (N25): But this one feels so happy...
Kanade: I took some time to think a little after we last spoke...
Kanade: All that joy I felt back then is never coming back, and I can't change what happened to my dad...
Kanade: But now...
Kanade: I'm okay with at least asking myself, “What if there's a way?”
KAITO (N25): ...
Kanade: So, I composed a song based on what I felt when I worked on my very first song...
Miku (N25): I see...
Kanade: *Nod* I don't plan on uploading it online since it feels a little too happy in terms of N25.
Rin (N25): What...?
Rin (N25): But... I really like this song.
Rin (N25): It'd be a shame if no one else gets to hear it...
Miku (N25): I agree.
Kanade: You think so...?
Rin (N25): Yeah, so you should have Mafuyu and the others also listen to it.
Kanade: Okay...
Ena: Huh? Kanade, you're already here?
Mizuki: What were you guys talking about?
Kanade: Oh, um. So...
Kanade: There's a song I'd like for you all to listen to.

Kanade's Father: ...
Kanade: I tried composing one based on what I felt back then...
