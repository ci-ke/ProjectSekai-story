123-6 How I Feel

(Character: Ichika Hoshino, Honami Mochizuki, Minori Hanasato, Airi Momoi, Kanade Yoisaki, Mafuyu Asahina, Ena Shinonome, Mizuki Akiyama, Hatsune Miku, Megurine Luka)

[Empty SEKAI]

Kanade: Let's see... Miku and the others should be...
Miku (N25): Oh, Kanade, welcome...
Kanade: Miku, Luka, perfect. I was just hoping to ask you both a question.
Miku (N25): A question?
Luka (N25): Oh, what would you like to ask us?
Kanade: Has anything been on either of your minds lately?
Luka (N25): Has anything been on our minds?
Kanade: Yes, for example... Have your shoulders been sore? Maybe you're not sleeping as well as you usually do...
Miku (N25): I'm not sure, but I don't think any of us have sore shoulders...
Kanade: I see... Then, what are your favorite foods?
Miku (N25): I'm happy with anything that you and the others bring over to SEKAI...
Miku (N25): But why do you ask?
Kanade: Uhh... So...
Luka (N25): Are you in the middle of choosing gifts for White Day?
Kanade: Huh? Oh...
Kanade: How did you know, Luka?
Luka (N25): Hehe. Mostly based on the time of year. Also, you were being very suspicious.
Kanade: R-Really...?
Miku (N25): Does this mean you're getting gifts for us?
Kanade: Yes, I am. I've been doing research by asking how everyone's been doing.
Miku (N25): Research?
Kanade: So, I've been having a lot of trouble deciding what to get everyone. So, I've been asking these questions to find gifts that you'll all be genuinely happy to receive.
Miku (N25): I see...
Luka (N25): So, do you already know what you're going to get everyone else?
Kanade: No, not yet.
Miku (N25): Oh, really?
Kanade: Yes. I've asked them if anything has been on their minds lately...
Kanade: But for example, I don't know what to get someone whose shoulders have been feeling a bit sore...
Miku (N25): Umm... Some medicine?
Kanade: Oh, what about a compress? Or a massage ball? They'd get to enjoy that for much longer...
Kanade: Bath salts might also be nice. A kind that helps improve blood circulation...
Miku (N25): That does sound nice. And warm...
Luka (N25): All of those seem like fun gift ideas.
Kanade: Really? Thank you.
Luka (N25): Yes, I'd like to see how everyone reacts when they see that you gave them a compress.
Kanade: Oh, right... I guess that would be a bit odd...
Luka (N25): Hehe. I'm just kidding. But it seems like you've already given this quite a bit of thought. You're still undecided?
Kanade: I've thought of a lot of ideas, but I haven't settled on any yet...
Kanade: I was really hoping that asking everyone would help me decide.
Luka (N25): Yes, I see.
Luka (N25): Then, I think the rest comes down to how you feel.
Kanade: What?
Luka (N25): If you're still torn between a couple of options after giving these gifts as much thought as you have, on top of going through the trouble of learning how everyone's doing...
Luka (N25): Then, all that's left is for you to go off gut feeling.
Miku (N25): Yes, I agree.
Miku (N25): How do you want them to feel, Kanade?
Kanade: How do I want them to feel...?
