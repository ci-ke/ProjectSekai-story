Mizuki: Oh, Saki, lemme help you!
Airi: Yeah, I'll give you a hand too!
Kanade: Blowing bubbles...? I haven't done that since I was little.
Mafuyu: Me neither...
Mafuyu: Oh...

Mafuyu: Thank you for asking. I think I will.
Saki: Yes, perfect! Here's a straw and bottle of bubble liquid!
Mafuyu: Okay, thank you.
Mafuyu: Here's your straw and bottle, Kanade.

Kanade: *Blow*

Mafuyu: *Blow*

Saki: Alright, you're on! Haruka, everyone, join us!
Haruka: Hehe. Sure.
Airi: Let's also join in!
Ena: Wait, including me? Alright, fine.
Ichika: Come to think of it, I remember managing to make double bubbles with a lot of effort.
Nene: Double bubbles?
Shiho: Oh, like blowing one inside another?
Ichika: Yeah, but I'd need one of those big bubble wands. I don't think I can with just a straw.
Honami: There might be one in that set Saki bought. Why don't we go ask her?
Ichika: Okay.
Mafuyu: Kanade? Is something wrong?
Kanade: Nothing's wrong. I was just seeing how much fun everyone's having.
Mafuyu: ...
Mafuyu: (I feel like blowing bubbles isn't something high schoolers should be doing...)
Mafuyu: (But...)
Airi: You're going down, Ena! *Inhale* *Blow*
Saki: Whoa! Airi, your bubble's huge...!
Mizuki: You can do it, Ena!
Ena: Alright... *Inhale* *Blow*
Minori: Let me try too! *Inhale*
Haruka: You need to blow before the liquid all drips out, Minori.
Ichika: Okay, watch. First, you make a big bubble using the wand.
Ichika: Then you blow a bubble inside it. *Blow* Oh no...
Nene: It popped. But you were really close.
Shiho: Yeah, I feel like you'll get it on your next try.
Ichika: Alright, one more time!
Honami: You can do it, Ichika!
Mafuyu: (They're all smiling and laughing...)
Mafuyu: (It's such a lively party, but it doesn't feel obnoxious...)
Mafuyu: (I guess this is okay once in a while...)
Mafuyu: *Blow*
(Black cut)
