Ichika: I knew it! It happens to me all the time whenever I try putting on my hoodie.
Honami: Hehe. It may just be the cost of having long hair.
Mafuyu: Maybe. I do sometimes wonder if I should try a shorter hairstyle...
Mafuyu: But not many people notice when I do cut my hair since it's already so long.
Ichika: Oh, it may be that only your closest friends are really able to see the difference.
Kanade: Sometimes it sounds so nice to have shorter hair. Life just seems so much easier...
Honami: You'd be saving quite a bit of time and money in terms of washing it. Also, less drying time.
Ichika: Oh, like during summer. It's awful when you end up sweating while your hair's still trying to dry just after washing it.
Ichika: And you have the longest hair out of all of us, Yoisaki. It must take quite a while for your hair to dry completely, no?
Kanade: I usually just try to wring it out as best I can and let it dry on its own after that.
Mafuyu: That might work during the summer, but you could catch a cold if you do that in winter, you know...?
Kanade: I know. I'm usually careful about that...
Kanade: To be honest, drying my hair isn't that much of a problem. Windy days are actually the worst for me.
Honami: Oh, because of your hair getting blown in your face?
Kanade: Yes, there's that. But also because of how I can't move forward when my hair gets blown back.
Kanade: Umm...
Mafuyu: Hehe. Kanade, I have to think you're the only person around who gets overwhelmed by the wind like that.
Kanade: R-Really? I thought everyone with long hair had this problem...
Kanade: Okay, what about this...?
Kanade: At night, you notice that your curtains are open a tiny bit and go to close them, but this long-haired girl suddenly appears...
Ichika: Yes! You're staggered for a moment, but you realize that it's just your reflection!
Kanade: Yes, thank you...! I'm glad you understand.
Mafuyu: Oh, that's right. You're not too fond of ghosts or anything like that.
Honami: Wait, is that true? I never would've thought.
Mafuyu: Hehe. She even mentioned this one time when she started hearing a strange noise while in her room.
Kanade: M-Mafuyu, that's...
Honami: I'm sort of curious now.
Ichika: Yes, me too.
Mafuyu: See? They want to know more.
Kanade: Y-Yes, I get that, but it's so embarrassing...
Mafuyu: Okay, fine. We'll keep it our little secret.
Kanade: Th-Thank you. Sorry about this, you two.
Ichika: No, it's been fun talking about our hair stories like this.
Honami: I agree. You have nothing to worry about.
Kanade: Thank goodness...
Kanade: (It's been so long since I last had a conversation like this with people outside of our group too...)
(Black cut)
