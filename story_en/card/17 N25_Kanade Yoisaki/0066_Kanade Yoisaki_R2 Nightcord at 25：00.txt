<Side Story (Part 1)>

(Character: Kanade Yoisaki, Mafuyu Asahina)

[Kanade's Room]

Kanade: <How's progress?>
(Background change)
Mafuyu: <Not too bad, could be better.>
Kanade: <...>
Kanade: <Hey...>
Mafuyu: <Yeah?>
Kanade: <Mind if I try calling you by your real name?>
Mafuyu: <Why would you want to do that?>
Kanade: <I just do.>
Mafuyu: <Okay then.>
Kanade: <Thanks.>
Kanade: <So... Mafuyu.>
Mafuyu: <Yes, Kanade?>
Kanade: <...>
Mafuyu: <How was it?>
Kanade: <What do you mean?>
Mafuyu: <Did it feel any different? You must have had a reason for wanting to use my real name instead of my handle.>
Kanade: <I don't know, it just came over me.>
Kanade: <I guess I...was curious.>
Mafuyu: <About me?>
Kanade: <Yeah. I want to know more about you, and Ena, and Mizuki too.>
Kanade: <I created Nightcord at 25:00 because I wanted to make music that some people might enjoy.>
Kanade: <I thought it didn't really matter who I was collaborating with as long as everyone knew how to do their part.>
Kanade: <But it does matter after all.>
Mafuyu: <...>
Kanade: <Since talking to you in Miku's SEKAI, I realized I wanted to get to know you all better.>
Mafuyu: <Because you want our group to stick together, making songs that will make people happy?>
Kanade: <That's only one part of the reason.>
Kanade: <I don't know if that's the right way to put it...>
Kanade: <But I think I just...want to be friends with you.>
Mafuyu: <Friends?>
Kanade: <Maybe I want to learn more about you guys because we share a destiny.>
Mafuyu: <Hehe, that makes more sense to me.>
Kanade: <So, Mafuyu... Tell me more about yourself.>
Mafuyu: <That's not a fair request.>
Mafuyu: <How can I tell you about myself when I don't really know what I am?>
Kanade: <You know how when you study, sometimes the way to understand something better is to try to explain it to someone else?>
Mafuyu: <Hehe. That may work for studying...>
Mafuyu: <But, for example, I couldn't even tell you why I laughed just now.>
Kanade: <Still...>
Kanade: <I just have this need to know more about you, so tell me anything. Describe your room to me.>
Mafuyu: <My room, huh. I'm sitting at my desk, and next to me are my textbooks, a dictionary, and a book.>
Kanade: <What book?>
Mafuyu: <Just something I borrowed from the library. I have to return it soon.>
Kanade: <Do you go to the library often?>
Mafuyu: <Not really. I borrowed this book because we had to read it for one class.>
Mafuyu: <Back to my room, there's a bed, a closet, the usual. And a fish tank.>
Kanade: <You're keeping pet fish?>
Mafuyu: <No.>
Mafuyu: <I just have some sand and aquatic plants in it.>
Kanade: <And what do you use it for?>
Mafuyu: <Nothing. I just like to space out looking at the plants swaying in the water.>
Kanade: <Ah.>
Mafuyu: <So? You got me all figured out now?>
Kanade: <Not yet...>
Kanade: <But you know what? This was the first time we talked at length about something other than music.>
Mafuyu: <Hehe. You're right.>
Mafuyu: <It might be nice to do it again sometime.>
Kanade: <Yeah.>
(Black cut)

[A few days later]

[Diner]

Mizuki: Have you decided what you're going to order? I'm getting cola and large fries!
Kanade: I'll have oolong tea.
Mafuyu: Same for me.
Ena: And I'll have...this limited edition dessert.
Mizuki: Of course you're going for that. It's gonna look good on your social media, am I right?
Ena: What do you care? Press the button and let's order.
Mizuki: Anyway... Kanade, what's this offline meeting for?
Kanade: I didn't have any special agenda for it. I just thought it might be nice to meet up.
Kanade: Knowing each other better might help us make better songs.
Mizuki: Um... I get it about wanting to make better music, but... You called us out here to socialize?
Kanade: Yeah.
Ena: Well, I'm kinda happy you did.
Kanade: Happy?
Ena: Yeah, that you're showing a real interest in us.
Mizuki: Normally, all you care about is music and making new songs.
Ena: I'd like to get to know you better too, Kanade. Can we ask you personal questions?
Kanade: Sure.
Ena: Yay! First, to get started, let me tell you a bit about myself.
Mizuki: She's got a brother one year younger than us. He's a bit cocky but does anything Ena says like a good boy.
Ena: Why are you butting in!? And you're just telling everyone about Akito, not me!
Mafuyu: So your brother's name is Akito?
Mizuki: Yeah! Guess what, he does music too!
Ena: That's enough about Akito, alright?!
Kanade: That's interesting to me too. I don't have any siblings, so I don't know what it's like.
Ena: Oh, I see... So, Akito's my younger brother, like Mizuki said...
Ena: We like and dislike the same foods. So we both like cheesecake and can't stand carrots.
Ena: Which is weird because in all other aspects we're very unlike one another.
Mizuki: Huh, you have such wholesome family relationships. I haven't even seen my sister in a long time.
Kanade: You have a sister?
Mizuki: Yeah, she's way older than me and lives abroad, so I don't really see her much.
Mizuki: We can text each other whenever, but I don't even remember when the last time we did that was.
Mafuyu: Seems like every family's different...
Mizuki: In case you were wondering, my favorite foods are curry rice and fries!
Mafuyu: Oh, that's what you ordered.
Ena: What's your favorite food, Mafuyu?
Mafuyu: I always tell people it's my mom's cooking.
Ena: That's what you tell people, but is it really?
Mafuyu: I don't really know what flavors I like.
Mizuki: What do you mean? You like the stuff that tastes good to you, simple as that, no?
Mafuyu: Tastes good?
Mafuyu: The thing is, I can't really tell what food tastes like. Like if it's good or bad.
Ena: What?! How does that even work?!
Mizuki: But, like, you can tell when something is salty, or what sort of texture it has, right? Try one of my fries and describe what you feel!
Mafuyu: But...they're your fries.
Mizuki: It's fine. I'm not going to eat them until they cool down a bit anyway, I'm sensitive to hot foods.
Kanade: So Ena and Mizuki have siblings, and Mafuyu doesn't know what foods she likes...
Mizuki: Kanade! Have some of my fries! Careful, though, they're really hot!
Kanade: Thanks.
(Black cut)
