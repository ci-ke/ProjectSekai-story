<Side Story (Part 1)>

(Character: Kanade Yoisaki, Mafuyu Asahina, Ena Shinonome, Mizuki Akiyama)

[Diner]

Mizuki: Great job, everyone♪ I think we really outdid ourselves this time too!
Ena: Thanks, Kanade, for uploading the song this morning.
Kanade: You're welcome.
Mizuki: Whoa, Kanade, you're staring a hole into that menu there. Did you skip meals again?
Kanade: I haven't eaten anything since yesterday, because I wanted to upload the song as soon as I could.
Ena: Then we should order quickly so that Kanade can get some food into her system soon.
Mizuki: Ah, I'll get some fries then. It shouldn't take long for them to be served♪
Mizuki: What would you like, Mafuyu?
Mafuyu: Anything...
Mizuki: Again? If anything's fine then just pick something.
Mafuyu: In that case... I'll get some fries too.
Mizuki: Okay, let's get a large serving and eat together! Excuse me, waiter! We'd like to order!
(Black cut)
Diner Staff: Thanks for waiting. Here's your extra large fries.
Mizuki: Food's here! Oh, but the fries are still piping hot.
Mizuki: Life's tough when you have a sensitive tongue... Oh, Kanade and Ena, feel free to help yourself♪
Kanade: Sure. Thanks.
Ena: Don't mind if I do!
Mafuyu: ...
Mizuki: What's wrong, Mafuyu?
Mafuyu: I taste nothing.
Mizuki: Oh... I know! Why don't we drizzle some soy sauce on them?
Ena: What are you talking about? If you do that, only Mafuyu will be able to eat the fries.
Ena: Also, the fries are already salted, so why are you trying to make them even saltier?
Ena: There are so many better seasonings we could eat the fries with, like avocado dips and cheese dips, you know?
Mizuki: Unfortunately, they don't provide that here. For condiments, there are only soy sauce and ketchup. Hey, how about we put even more salt on the fries, then?
Mafuyu: I'm fine with anything. It'll just taste the same to me, anyway.
Ena: You may be fine, but I'm not!
Kanade: I saw a few dipping sauces on the menu just now. Should we order some?
Ena: So they do have dipping sauces here! Why were you trying to put us through that risky taste experiment, then?!
Mizuki: Not at all! We should absolutely try figuring something out with just the condiments available on the table!
Ena: What's the point?
Kanade: Hehe...
Mizuki: ...
Mizuki: (Well, I guess the mood's definitely better than before.)
Mizuki: (We've all got stuff going on in our lives, so it's not like we can be normal friends, but...)
Mafuyu: ...
Mizuki: (Getting to know Mafuyu better through the doll exhibition was a major milestone.)
Mizuki: (I'm glad I got everyone to go, even if I had to be a little forceful about it. We're not so awkward around each other anymore.)
Mizuki: I'm such a thoughtful and considerate person! Whoa, hang on!
Mizuki: Hey! Why're the fries already almost gone? I was just waiting for them to cool down a little!
Ena: You told us to help ourselves.
Mizuki: Yes, but you'd usually hold back a little, right?!
Ena: It's hard to stop once you start, especially for fries.
Kanade: Yes, I find it intriguing.
Mizuki: Not you too, Kanade... Wait! Of course. Kanade's stomach was completely empty just now!
Kanade: Yes. But I'm full now.
Mafuyu: ...
Mizuki: Ah... There goes most of my fries... Mafuyu, you should really stop eating with such a businesslike face.
Ena: Do you want this instead?
Mizuki: Oh, Ena, you're so nice! But that's lasagna, right? You know I can't eat food that's too hot!
Mizuki: Hot food tears the skin in my mouth to shreds!
Ena: Don't make it sound so gross.
Mafuyu: Kanade, your order is here.
Kanade: Thanks.
Ena: The plate of fries is kind of blocking the table. Let's get the waiter to clear it for us.
Mizuki: What? Ah! My fries! They're all gone!
Mizuki: Waiter! Another large portion of fries please!
Mizuki: Oh, and when it's ready, could you please let it cool down a little before bringing it out? Thanks...
(Black cut)
