<Side Story (Part 1)>

(Character: Hatsune Miku, Kagamine Rin, Kagamine Len, Megurine Luka, MEIKO, KAITO)

[School SEKAI]

Rin (Ln): Shih and the girls are working really hard again today!
MEIKO (Ln): The show is just around the corner. I'm sure they're busy putting on the finishing touches.
KAITO (Ln): I'm sure they're going to put on a good performance.
Rin (Ln): Me too☆ I really can't wait!
MEIKO (Ln): Still, I know they're the opening act rather than the headliners, but they really get to perform at that big arena...
KAITO (Ln): It seats fifteen thousand, right? I also can't believe they get to play in front of such a large audience.
MEIKO (Ln): I'm still having trouble processing that number. What do you think it's going to be like for them?
Rin (Ln): I bet the crowd's gonna roar when they play! I'm really jealous of how much fun they're gonna have!
KAITO (Ln): I might get a little nervous...
MEIKO (Ln): Really? I feel like you'd get into a nice groove once the show started.
MEIKO (Ln): That being said, Ichika and the girls are dealing with quite a bit of pressure. It sounds like the band they're opening for is very famous.
Rin (Ln): Oh, right... It's not all fun and games for them.
Rin (Ln): I know! What if we came up with a magic spell to help them feel better?!
KAITO (Ln): A magic spell?
Rin (Ln): Yeah! That way, if they get nervous on stage they don't have to worry!
MEIKO (Ln): I see what you mean. In that case, why don't the three of us try coming up with one together?
Rin (Ln): Woohoo!
Rin (Ln): Hmm... How to feel less nervous...
Rin (Ln): Oh, I know! How about smiling from ear to ear?
Rin (Ln): A big smile like this! I'm feeling better already!
MEIKO (Ln): Haha. That's not a bad idea. It makes me want to smile too, so it helps others relax as well.
KAITO (Ln): But won't it be a bit difficult to smile when you're feeling nervous?
Rin (Ln): Oh, right! It's hard to smile when you're all frozen up...
Rin (Ln): So, scratch that!
KAITO (Ln): Come to think of it, I think one of the magazines Shiho lent us said something about dealing with nervousness...
MEIKO (Ln): Oh, that might be just the hint we need.
Rin (Ln): You gotta remember what it said, KAITO!
KAITO (Ln): It involved drawing people on your palm or something... And eating them?
Rin (Ln): What?! You have to eat people?!
MEIKO (Ln): Oh, I think I've heard that before somewhere. You just pretend to gobble them up, yes?
Rin (Ln): I've never heard of that before! Lemme give that a try!
Rin (Ln): Let's see... Pretend to draw some people on my palm...
KAITO (Ln): Okay, that's three... No, I'll draw five...
Rin (Ln): Forget it. I'm gonna draw as many as I can, and... Nom!
Rin (Ln): Hmm... I don't think it worked? I'm not really sure though.
MEIKO (Ln): Well, these kinds of things probably don't work very well if you don't understand the meaning behind them.
MEIKO (Ln): So, why don't we try a different idea? For example, we can think about us instead.
Rin (Ln): Us?
MEIKO (Ln): Yes. When you're feeling nervous, Rin, what would help make you feel better?
Rin (Ln): Hmm... If I were feeling nervous...
Rin (Ln): Oh! Having you and KAITO next to me would do the trick, MEIKO!
Rin (Ln): Knowing that people I love are right there next to me would make me feel invincible☆
MEIKO (Ln): Haha. I know what you mean.
KAITO (Ln): Then, why don't we think of a spell that makes it feel like the people you care about are with you?
Rin (Ln): Those we care about...
Rin (Ln): Oh, what if we modified the thing we just did?!
Rin (Ln): Instead of drawing people, you could write the names of people you really care about and gobble those up!
KAITO (Ln): Huh...?
MEIKO (Ln): Their names...?
Rin (Ln): Yeah, like this!
Rin (Ln): I'll write your name, MEIKO, since you're always hanging out with me and giving me great advice! And your name, KAITO, since you're quiet but super reliable!
Rin (Ln): Next, Luka, because she's so nice and always watches over me♪ And Miku-P because she does such a great job of leading us!
Rin (Ln): Oh, and Len since we always practice and have contests together and stuff!
Rin (Ln): Nom...!
Rin (Ln): (Oh...!)
