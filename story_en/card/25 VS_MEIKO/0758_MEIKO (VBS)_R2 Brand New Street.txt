[Street SEKAI]

[Crase Cafe]

KAITO (VBS): And it sure will be! It's a dish that will have all of the customers' favorites on it.

Rin (VBS): An likes rum raisin ice cream, right? So if she orders that, then she'd end up with ice cream for a meal.
