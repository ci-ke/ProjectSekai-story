Miku (N25): She never used to let anybody into this SEKAI. But now...you're all here.
Miku (N25): You all have a place in Mafuyu's heart.
Mizuki: We have a place in...

Kanade: You're welcome.
Ena: It doesn't feel that way to me at all, though.
Mizuki: Maybe it's just that Mafuyu hasn't noticed it herself, yet?
Miku (N25): So please keep looking for Mafuyu together with her, now and in the future.
Kanade: You have my word. I'll definitely save her.
Mizuki: Yup! We'll fill this SEKAI up with all sorts of different things, like this marionette!
Ena: Eh, I wouldn't mind a less pesky version of her, I guess.
